EVENTS

Technical

Events

The technical events include hackathons, games related to coding, and some minor inter-college competitions like multiplayer gaming. All the techies out there, GATES is all set for you!

Cultural

Events

The cultural events include dance performances by different students, like Bhangra and western dances, and music performances and competitions between students of various colleges. Students get exposure and exciting prizes are won by them.

Management

Events

Management and Literary events include competitions like Bulls n Bear which is a spelling competition sessions where the students are updated about the stock markets and then given life-like situations on how to handle stocks.

Miscellaneous

Events

Miscellaneous events include the other fun events of GATES such as Selfie to fame, Game of trust and many more! Come join us and enjoy the most of it!

Divine

Events

Divine events include the other devotional events of GATES such as Gatka Competition, Fashion Walk, Gyan Bodh and many more! Come join us and enjoy the most of it!

ABOUT


Our aim is to carve a niche for GATES that would set it apart from all other fest in India. We stand by GATES' motto of fun and success. and wish to showcase the uniqueness which GTBIT stands by hosting innovative,groundbreaking and progressive events. We envisioned this festival as a platform, or medium through which students could showcase their talent and culture
